Experience

I am currently working at the Jean Mayer USDA Human Nutrition Research Center on Aging at Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts, I work as a Clinical Research Assistant. In this role, I am responsible for maintaining documentation of participant diets and exercise, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. I also collect vital signs and supervise participants involved in the NIH Nutrition for Precision Health Study.

Additionally, in the summer of 2023, I interned remotely at Synterex in Dedham, Massachusetts, as a Science Communication Intern. My tasks included performing literature searches in PubMed to gather information on various health topics. I translated complex medical information into accessible language for educational social media posts and blog articles. I also created infographics and videos to clarify the content. Furthermore, I used EndNote for referencing documents, edited regulatory materials, and participated in team meetings, where I prepared the meeting minutes.
